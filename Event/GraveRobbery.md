# Grave Robbery

![v0.1](/Images/Grave_Robbery_v0.1.png)

## Strategy

Allows for consistent buys from the trash at a discount.
Synergizes with means to trash desirable cards, such as [Lurker][^1] or
[Bandit][^2].
